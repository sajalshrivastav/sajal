## Quick Setup:

### Step 1: Get Free Access Key
1. Go to [Web3Forms.com](https://web3forms.com/)
2. Enter your email: `ss.official2018@gmail.com`
3. Click "Create Access Key"
4. Copy the access key (looks like: `abc123-def456-ghi789`)

### Step 2: Update Contact.jsx
Replace this line in `src/components/Contact.jsx`:
```javascript

```

With your real access key:
